About Konnektid

What do you want to learn? What can you teach someone? That's what we would like to know at Konnektid. By connecting yourself to people you don't know yet but who can teach you something, you are helping each other develop and you will get to know who is nearby. We'll enable you to discover the social capital around you.

The Team

Konnektid is developed by Simone Potenza and Michel Visser. We are a small team, but we want to grow. Within the coming years we want to be known as the application for finding knowledge and skills in your immediate vicinity. Help yourself and others develop and get things done. Konnektid is innovative. We want to surprise our users with a smart application. Together with VU Amsterdam we can build a smart algorithm.
